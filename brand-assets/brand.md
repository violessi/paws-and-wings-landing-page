**Brand Name**
Paw & Wings Pet Care Hub

**One-line Description (What does it do?)**
A one-stop veterinary, grooming, wellness, and pet supply center for pets of all kinds.

**Audience (Who's it for?)**
Pet owners seeking trusted care, products, and services for dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, rabbits, hamsters, and other companion animals.

**Brand Colors (Hex)**

* Deep Veterinary Green — **#0F5D52**
* Warm Peach — **#F29D7A**
* Soft Sage — **#A8C89B**

This combination feels:

* Professional enough for a veterinary clinic
* Friendly enough for grooming and retail
* Modern without looking corporate
* Inclusive of both traditional and exotic pets

The green builds trust, the peach adds warmth, and the sage keeps the brand soft and approachable.
