01 — About

One roof for every pet, big, small, feathered, or scaled.

Paw & Wings is a one-stop veterinary, grooming, wellness, and pet supply center for pets of all kinds — from dogs and cats to birds, reptiles, rabbits, and hamsters.

We bring together professional veterinary care, friendly grooming services, and a curated selection of quality supplies under one roof, so pet owners can give their companions the best in health and happiness.
