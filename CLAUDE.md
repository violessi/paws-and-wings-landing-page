# Project: Paw & Wings Landing Page

## Frontend workflow (mandatory)

- **Always invoke the `frontend-design` skill before writing any front-end code.** This applies to HTML, CSS, JS, and component files. No exceptions, even for small edits.
- **Use the screenshot loop to verify visual work** — after rendering changes, capture and review the output, iterate until it matches intent.

## Brand reference

- All logos, colors, and brand guidelines live in `brand-assets/`:
  - `brand-assets/brand.md` — brand name, tagline, audience, hex colors
  - `brand-assets/ChatGPT Image Jun 12, 2026, 03_05_59 AM.png` — logo
- Use the documented hex values directly; do not invent palette colors.

## Design principles

- Clean and modern. Avoid the generic "AI vibe coded" look: no gratuitous gradients on every surface, no overuse of glassmorphism, no emoji-heavy headings, no purple-to-pink default palettes, no centered-everything layouts by default.
- Favor restrained typography, intentional whitespace, and the brand palette over decorative flourishes.
- Choices should be defensible: every color, spacing value, and component pattern should have a reason tied to the brand or the content.
